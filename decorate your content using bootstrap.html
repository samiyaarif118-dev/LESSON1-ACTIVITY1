Gardening

is the practice of growing and caring for plants, flowers, and vegetables in home gardens and outdoor spaces to enhance beauty and sustainability. At the centre of gardening is a 
well-prepared soil bed
with proper nutrients and moisture, each garden comprising healthy roots supported by water and sunlight. The gardener improves growth by planting seeds and tending plants with the right tools, while the maintenance and care side tries to protect plants and
nurture
them to maturity.
