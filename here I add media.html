My Favorite Animal: The Tiger

The tiger is one of the most powerful and beautiful animals in the world. It lives in forests and grasslands. Tigers are known for their orange fur with black stripes. They are strong hunters and can run very fast.

Interesting Facts About Tigers

  • Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world.
  • They can weigh up to 300 kilograms.
