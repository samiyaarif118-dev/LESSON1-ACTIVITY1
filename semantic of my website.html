History of cricket

Cricket originated in England in the 16th century and has evolved into an international sport with professional leagues worldwide.Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world, loved by millions of fans across continents. It is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams, usually of eleven players each. Cricket matches can vary in format, ranging from the fast-paced T20 games that last a few hours, to One Day Internationals (ODIs) which last a full day, and the traditional Test matches played over five days.
